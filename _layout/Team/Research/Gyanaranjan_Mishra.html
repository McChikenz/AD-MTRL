Gyanaranjan Mishra (PhD Student)

I am a 2nd year PhD student in the Alloy Design and Materials Testing Research Laboratory under the Mechanical Engineering department. I earned my bachelor's from the National Institute of Technology, Rourkela, and my master's from the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay—both in Metallurgical and Materials Science Engineering. I received the institute medal for being the best metallurgist during my bachelor's. I previously worked as a principal researcher in the R&D department of Tata Steel Limited, India, focusing on new product development for automotive, line pipe, and construction applications. Currently, I collaborate with Natural Resources Canada – CanmetMATERIALS to develop high-performance electrical steel for aero-engine applications. Outside of research, I’m an avid saltwater aquarist and orchidist.
